What this policy covers

This policy explains what we collect when you use our website and test app, how we use that information, and the choices you have. It applies to skydeal.co.in and any subdomains we operate.

Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Inputs for fare estimation such as origin/destination, dates, cabin class, passenger count, and chosen payment methods.
  • Contact information if you email us (e.g., your email address and message).

Information collected automatically

  • Standard web logs (IP address, browser type, referrer/exit pages, timestamps) captured by our hosting provider for security and reliability.
  • Basic device information for performance/debugging.

Derived data

We compute price estimates by applying public offers to baseline fares returned by flight search providers.

Cookies & analytics

We aim to operate with minimal cookies. If we enable analytics in the future, we will use privacy-respecting, first-party solutions and update this section. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

Sharing & transfers

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers who help us operate the site, under contracts that restrict their use of the data.

  • Hosting & deploy: Vercel (site) and our chosen backend host (for API).
  • Flight data providers: When you run a search, our backend may call flight content providers to retrieve fares (e.g., GDS/NDC/aggregators). Data sent is limited to search parameters (routes, dates, cabin, pax). No payment/card data is collected by us.
  • Click-outs: If you click out to an OTA/airline, you’ll be subject to that site’s privacy policy.
  • Legal: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and integrity of our service.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, then delete or anonymize it. Email correspondence may be retained for records and support.

Your choices

  • Do not email: You can choose not to contact us or request deletion of your messages.
  • Opt-out of non-essential cookies: Use your browser’s cookie controls.
  • Access/Deletion: Email contact@skydeal.co.in to request a copy or deletion of your data we hold (subject to legal limits).

Children

Our service is not directed to children under 16. If you believe a child provided us information, contact us and we’ll delete it.

International users
