About SkyDeal

Final-price estimation for flights in India

What we do

SkyDeal estimates the final checkout price on popular Indian OTAs by applying applicable bank / UPI / wallet offers to live fares. Our goal is simple: no surprises at payment.

How it works

  • Fetch baseline fares from flight content providers
  • Match public offers to payment methods you select
  • Compute your expected payment-page total

What we are not

  • We don’t issue tickets on this site
  • We don’t collect or process card details
  • We don’t sell your data—see our privacy policy
