About SkyDeal

We show true checkout prices for Indian OTAs by applying real bank/UPI/wallet offers to live fares—matched to the payment methods you can actually use.

What we do

  • Compare across OTAs on the final price you pay, not just base fare.
  • Apply real offers (with caps & terms) to every search.
  • Let you pick the cards/UPI/wallets you actually have.
  • Explain the math—no hidden discounts or surprise fees.

Why it matters

  • Different payment methods unlock different “best” portals.
  • Screenshot deals expire; we keep offers current and auditable.
  • Transparent savings: every rupee accounted for, before checkout.

Status & roadmap

  • Private beta: search/booking not enabled on this site yet.
  • Expanding bank/UPI/wallet coverage and OTA integrations.
  • Performance, UX and explainability polishing.

Who we are

A small, product-first team building from India. If you care about clear pricing and neat engineering, we’d love to hear from you.
